where global is local

Located in the heart of Philadelphia’s Olney neighborhood, North 5th Street is brimming with over 360 unique businesses, and an exceptional variety of restaurants. The incredible array of exciting new places you’ll find along North 5th Street is a reflection of the diversity of the many countries our residents represent. “North 5th Street, Where Global is Local”
Olney offers affordable houses in excellent condition, quality public and parochial schools, and unbeatable parks. It’s the perfect place to call home or visit and try something new every day!
